Why we asked this...
This kind of problem is very common – you have a certain amount of money, you spend some of it, and you need to work out how much is left. This sort of calculation is essential if you want to manage your money!
The techniques that we will look at will work for any problem that involves subtraction. These problems could include:Working out how much of a journey is left after you have covered a certain distanceUsing meter readings to find out how many units of gas or electricity you have used.Calculating how much more money you need to save to reach a certain target.
When would I need this?
Because subtraction is useful in so many different situations, it is worth making the effort to understand how to do it. Take your time, and work through the different methods. You don’t need to use all of them - the important thing is to find a method that works reliably for you.
