The answer is 12˚C. Hit Next to see how we did it.

Our starting temperature is -7˚C; that's 7 degrees below zero. We need to extend our temperature scale to the left. Notice where we mark our starting temperature - it's a negative temperature, so it goes seven places to the left of zero.

The temperature went up by 19˚C altogether. The first 7˚C increase took us to 0˚C.
There are another 12 degrees to go (because 7 + 12 = 19). That takes us to 12˚C.
